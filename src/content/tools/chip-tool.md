The Matter controller/commissioner CLI from the connectedhomeip SDK. Commissions a Matter device over Bluetooth LE — establishing a PASE (SPAKE2+) session from the setup passcode, handing over Thread/Wi-Fi credentials — then drives it with CASE-secured operational commands. The tool for assessing Matter onboarding and the application-layer cluster interface.
