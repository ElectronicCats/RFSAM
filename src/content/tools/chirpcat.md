LoRa/LoRaWAN discovery, classification and orchestration platform. Listens to traffic via a gateway backend (Semtech UDP packet forwarder), clusters packets by RF characteristics, scores/classifies targets and dispatches follow/capture tasks to CatSniffer workers. Designed for SX130x gateways (RAK WisGate / CM4).
